Tokens

I had noticed that all game objects coordinates (X, Y, and Year) were visible within BurpSuite. Unfortunately, I couldn’t get FireBug to output my characters current X, Y position which made finding tokens problematic. I decided to use NPC’s as a point of reference and went old-school cartographer mode and created a map.

Once completing the challenge, I decided to debug the JavaScript and enable my character to teleport around. I was only semi-successful with teleportation but I was able to make my character faster and specify coordinates to walk to. Video below of how I went about grabbing tokens:
